Peanut Butter and Jelly Cookies

HOW TO MAKE THESE PEANUT BUTTER AND JELLY COOKIES

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the almond flour, maple syrup, vanilla, melted coconut oil, and salt until a dough forms.
  3. Shape the dough into five balls and place them on a baking sheet, leaving 1" between each ball. Flatten the balls into a disc.
  4. Bake for about 10 minutes, until lightly golden on the edges. Cool completely.
  5. Spread an even layer of jelly on top of each cooled cookie.
  6. Mix peanut butter, maple syrup, and coconut oil (if using), then spread over the jelly.
  7. Melt chocolate chips and coconut oil in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring frequently until smooth.
  8. Spread an even layer of chocolate on top of the peanut butter.
  9. Freeze the cookies for about an hour and enjoy!

INGREDIENTS

  1. Cookie
    • 1 cup almond flour
    • 3 tbsp maple syrup
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla
    • 2 tbsp coconut oil, melted
    • Pinch of salt
  1. Jelly
    • 1/4 cup jelly
  1. Peanut Butter
    • 1/4 cup peanut butter or almond butter
    • Optional: 1 tbsp maple syrup
    • Optional: 1 tbsp coconut oil
  1. Chocolate
    • 1 cup chocolate chips
    • 1 tbsp coconut oil

So, how do you make these healthy peanut butter and jelly cookies?

  • Preheat oven and prepare the baking sheet.
  • Mix ingredients to form cookie dough.
  • Shape into balls, flatten, and bake.
  • Cool completely before adding layers of jelly, peanut butter, and chocolate.
