Honey Caramel Rice Krispie Treats

HOW TO MAKE THESE CARAMEL RICE KRISPIES

  • The full instructions for these homemade rice krispies are below, but here’s the gist of it:

  • First, toast the puffed rice cereal, rice krispies treat cereal, and oats for about 15 minutes until lightly toasted. Then, heat coconut milk, honey, and butter over in a pan for about 5-6 minutes until the mixture thickens. This is our caramel!

  • Add the homemade marshmallows, vanilla, and salt. Mix in the toasted cereals and oats until evenly coated.

  • Transfer the mixture to a baking pan and let the mixture cool to room temperature. Cut into squares, and store in an airtight container for about 3 days.

KEY INGREDIENTS

  • Puffed rice cereal
  • Rice Krispies Treats cereal
  • Old fashioned oats
  • Butter (substitute: Earth Balance butter if vegan)
  • Coconut milk
  • Honey
  • Marshmallows
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
