Hearts of Palm Pasta Recipe

HOW TO MAKE CARAMELIZED ONION HEARTS OF PALM PASTA

  • Sauté the Hearts of Palm Pasta: Heat a skillet over high heat and drizzle in olive oil. Add the hearts of palm pasta and toss to coat. Cook for about 5 minutes, allowing it to heat through and absorb the oil’s flavor.
  • Caramelize the Shallots and Garlic: Add in the remaining oil and butter, along with the sliced shallots and minced garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Cook on low for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the shallots are soft and golden. Remember, low and slow is the way to go for caramelization!
  • Create the Sauce: Once the shallots are perfectly caramelized, season them and pour in the heavy cream (if using) and quickly mix it to create a creamy sauce. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed—don’t be shy!
  • Combine and Serve: Add the hearts of palm pasta back into the skillet. Toss everything together until the pasta is well-coated with the sauce. Serve hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese for that perfect finishing touch.

KEY INGREDIENTS FOR MY HEARTS OF PALM PASTA RECIPE

  • Olive oil, or sun dried tomato oil
  • Hearts of palm pasta
  • Seasonings
  • Shallots
  • Heavy cream (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (for serving)
  • Garlic
