Tahini Basil Chickpea Wraps

The full recipe for my rosemary garlic muffins is below—it’s super easy! But here’s the gist of it:

  • Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a baking sheet with foil and spray with non-stick spray.
  • Add tahini basil dressing ingredients to a small bowl. Mix until combined. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  • Toss the chickpeas in the dressing, ensuring even coating.
  • Roast in the oven for 20 minutes, stirring halfway for even browning.
  • Warm the flatbread in the oven until soft and pliable.
  • Assemble wraps by spreading tahini dressing, adding lettuce, tomatoes, and chickpeas.
  • Drizzle with remaining dressing, serve warm, and enjoy!

TAHINI BASIL BEANS INGREDIENTS

  • Garlic
  • Chickpeas
  • Basil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seasonings
  • Tahini
