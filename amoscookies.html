Healthy Famous Amos Cookies

Which cookies are healthiest?

  • By definition, cookies are a dessert which means they contain some sugar. There are creative cookie recipes out there that use a non-traditional sweeteners such as banana, but that type of substitute doesn’t work for this particular recipe.

  • But, the main swaps we make for this healthy Famous Amos cookies recipe is using coconut sugar instead of white or brown sugar. While these healthy cookies are not low in sugar, coconut sugar overall is better for your blood sugar.

  • You can also use sugar free chocolate chips to lower the amount of sugar in this recipe even more!

What are the ingredients for these healthy chocolate chip cookies?

  1. Cookies
    • 1/2 c coconut oil, melted
    • 1/2 c coconut sugar
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla
    • 1/4 tsp baking soda
    • 1/4 tsp baking powder
    • Pinch of salt
    • 2 tsp non-dairy milk
    • 1/3 c regular or sugar-free chocolate chips, roughly chopped*

So, how do you make these healthy chocolate chip cookies?

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the melted coconut oil, coconut sugar, and vanilla until creamy.
  3. Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and non-dairy milk. Mix until a dough starts to form.
  4. Fold in the chopped chocolate chips.
  5. Roll the dough into small teaspoon-sized balls. Lightly press the dough down to flatten, and bake the cookies for about 12-15 minutes. Cookies will become crispier as they cool.
