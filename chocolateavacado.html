Chocolate Avocado Tarts

What is a chocolate avocado tart?

These delightful dessert tarts combine two flavors that you don’t always see together: chocolate and avocado. The crunchy chocolate crust forms the base of these tarts while the avocado filling has a pudding-like texture that firms up in the freezer. These vegan chocolate avocado tarts are a unique and tasty little treat!

INGREDIENTS

  1. Chocolate Crust
    • 1 c almond flour
    • 1/4 c cocoa powder
    • 2 tbsp coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
    • 2 tbsp maple syrup
    • Pinch of salt
  1. Filling
    • 1 avocadoer
    • 1/2-3/4 c maple syrup
    • 1/3-1/2 c nut butter of your choice (I like cashew butter)
    • Splash of vanilla

Instructions

  • Chocolate Crust
    1. Grease the insides of a mini muffin pan with coconut oil.
    2. Mix all of the chocolate crust ingredients together in a large bowl. It might be easier to start with a spoon and then use your hands as the dough will be thick.
    3. Divide the dough into 12 equal balls (about 1 tbsp each). Press each dough ball inside the muffin pan, covering the bottom and sides.
    4. Place the muffin pan in the freezer while you prepare the filling.
  • Filling
    1. Use a food processor to blend all of the filling ingredients together, starting with the lesser amounts of maple syrup and nut butter. It should come together quickly, and the filling should be very smooth and creamy.
    2. Taste the filling and add more maple syrup to increase the sweetness, if necessary, or more nut butter to conceal the avocado taste if it comes through.
  • Assembly
    1. Spoon about 1-2 tsp of the filling inside of each crust, smoothing down the top so it's flat.
    2. Freeze for 1 hour.
    3. Use a knife to gently loosen each chocolate avocado tart from the sides of the muffin pan. It should come out easily if you greased the pan.
    4. Enjoy cold
