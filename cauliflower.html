Chickpea Cauliflower Flatbread

HOW TO MAKE THESE CHICKPEA CAULIFLOWER FLATBREAD

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until soft, about 3-5 minutes. Now, add the chickpeas and cauliflower rice, and cook for another 5 minutes, until the veggies are soft and heated through.
  2. Sprinkle the seasonings on the veggies and stir until evenly distributed. Taste and add more salt, if needed.
  3. To make the tahini dressing: mix all ingredients together in a small bowl.
  4. To assemble: spread hummus on one side of the flatbread and top with a scoop of the chickpea and cauliflower filling. Fold the flatbread in half, like a taco.
  5. Heat a cast iron skillet or saucepan over high heat, and press the folded flatbread in the pan until it's brown and crispy. Flip and repeat on the other side.
  6. Open up the flatbread and add some lettuce and tomato, if using. Drizzle the tahini dressing on top and serve.

INGREDIENTS

  1. filling
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 can of chickpeas, drained, rinsed, and lightly mashed
    • 2 c cauliflower rice
    • 1/2 onion, chopped
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • 1/2 tsp coriander
    • pinches of black pepper, and cayenne pepper
  1. Tahini Dressing
    • 1/4 c tahini
    • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 tsp mustard
    • 2 - 4 tbsp water
    • Pinch of black pepper
    • 1 tsp salt

Is this recipe vegan?

  • Yup! This recipe is completely vegan. From the chickpea and cauliflower filling to the homemade tahini dressing, there are no animal products in this recipe at all. The flatbread I used is vegan, but be sure to check the ingredients of your flatbread if you buy it from a store.
