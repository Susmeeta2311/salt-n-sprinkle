Double Chocolate Protein Bars

DOUBLE CHOCOLATE PROTEIN BAR RECIPE

  • Mix the protein bar ingredients in a small bowl.
  • Spread the brownie mixture in a loaf pan and chill for an hour until firm.
  • Melt the dark chocolate chips and dip each bar, coating evenly.
  • Place the bars on a parchment-lined baking sheet and chill again until set.

BROWNIE PROTEIN BAR INGREDIENTS

  • Almond Butter
  • Maple Syrup
  • Oat Flour
  • Protein Powder
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Dark Chocolate Chips

ARE THESE PROTEIN BARS HEALTHY?

  • Rather than labeling foods as healthy or unhealthy, focus on balanced eating with whole foods.
  • Store-bought protein bars are processed and contain artificial sweeteners.
  • This homemade recipe offers a healthier alternative with wholesome ingredients.
